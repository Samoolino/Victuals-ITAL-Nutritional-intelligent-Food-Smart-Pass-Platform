# Role Model and Governance

## Core roles
- User: receives nutrition access and manages plan entitlements
- Market Owner / Market Hub: operates inventory, redemption, and settlement workflows
- Subscription Plan Owner: funds plans and defines policy parameters
- Plan Subscriber: participates in a plan and consumes nutrition allocation
- Platform Administrator: governs operations, compliance, and platform configuration

## Governance principles
- role separation and auditable approvals
- policy-driven access and settlement
- transparent impact reporting
- traceable contract and service actions

## Naming standard
The platform should consistently use this vocabulary across code, APIs, contracts, docs, and user interfaces.
