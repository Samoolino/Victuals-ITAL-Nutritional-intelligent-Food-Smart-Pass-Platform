# Nutrition Intelligence Service

The nutrition intelligence service hosts the ITAL reasoning stack for nutritional planning, diagnostic inference, product suitability, meal design, and plan recommendation logic.

## Responsibilities
- orchestrate ITAL agents and specialized nutrition workflows
- evaluate user profiles against nutrition targets and medical constraints
- score product suitability and plan eligibility
- generate explainable recommendations for users and plan owners

## Alignment
This service is the intelligence core of the Victuals platform.
