# Market Hub Portal

The market hub portal is the operational surface for market owners and market hubs. It allows them to manage inventory, product eligibility, pricing, redemption intake, settlement status, and merchant reporting.

## Responsibilities
- onboard and manage market inventory
- approve product eligibility against plan rules
- receive redemption requests from users
- track settlement and marketplace performance

## Alignment
This module sits on top of the registry, marketplace, and settlement services and is central to the commerce layer.
